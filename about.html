About Aling Nena Store

Aling Nena Grocery Store


Located in the heart of Muntinlupa City, Aling Nena Grocery Store is more than just a retail spot—it is a community landmark. We pride ourselves on providing high-quality household essentials, favorite Filipino snacks (from Piattos to Stik-O), and refreshing beverages at prices that respect the hard-earned peso of every Muntinlupeno.


Our store operates on the values of trust and neighborhood spirit, embodied by our famous mantra: "Bayad muna bago utang!" (Pay first before credit!).
